Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 31, 2025

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how the YouTube Analytics Dashboard ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our service. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your data.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Authentication Data

When you sign in with your Google account, we collect:

  • Your Google account email address
  • OAuth tokens for accessing YouTube APIs
  • Session information to maintain your login state

2.2 YouTube Data

With your permission, we access and display:

  • Your YouTube channel information (name, subscriber count, thumbnail)
  • Video metadata (titles, thumbnails, publish dates, duration)
  • Video analytics (views, likes, average view duration, watch percentage)
  • Subscriber gain data (if available)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Authenticate your access to the dashboard
  • Fetch and display your YouTube video analytics
  • Provide sorting and filtering functionality
  • Cache data to improve performance
  • Maintain your session across browser visits

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Local Storage

Your data is stored locally on our servers:

  • OAuth tokens are stored securely in server sessions
  • Video analytics data is cached locally for 6 hours
  • No data is stored in external databases

4.2 Security Measures

We implement the following security measures:

  • HTTPS encryption for all data transmission
  • Secure session management
  • Automatic token refresh and validation
  • Regular cache cleanup of old data

5. Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or rent your personal information to third parties. Your data is used solely for providing the YouTube Analytics Dashboard service.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your stored data
  • Request deletion of your cached data
  • Revoke access to your YouTube account
  • Log out and clear your session

7. Third-Party Services

Our service integrates with:

  • Google OAuth: For secure authentication
  • YouTube Data API v3: For video metadata
  • YouTube Analytics API v2: For video analytics

These services have their own privacy policies. We recommend reviewing Google's Privacy Policy for information about how they handle your data.

8. Data Retention

We retain your data as follows:

  • Session data: Until you log out or the session expires (24 hours)
  • Cached analytics: 6 hours from the last fetch
  • OAuth tokens: Until you revoke access or they expire

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
